20th July,2015

Each day a bright one,
Followed by the good old Sun.
While nature's joy swells up to the skies,
"Oh it's morning already!" our worked up world sighs.

Slowly the chirps and chaos begin to fade,
The busy bees get along with the life they have made.
Treating their young ones with love and care,
Oh look, there is the mother dove.

The day goes by and the evening comes
The sharpness of the old Sun is now blunt
The waves keep making the sea foam
And all are now heading to home.

And by the time a baby sparrow got lost,
Some of his mates flew down to the Aust.
Not everyone has returned home happy and bright,
Few sailed through while few lost the light.

But won't the Sun turn again full bright?
Nothing stays permamently out of light.
The flora, the fauna welcomes it with joy and pride!
"Oh it's morning again!" But the humans sigh.

- Aafrin Ansari
